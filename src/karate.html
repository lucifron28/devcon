Training and Techniques

Karate training is divided into three main sections: Kihon (basics), Kata (forms), and Kumite (sparring). Each section is crucial for developing the skills and discipline required in karate.

  • Kihon: Basic techniques and movements, including stances, strikes, kicks, and blocks.
  • Kata: Choreographed patterns of movements that demonstrate various offensive and defensive techniques.
  • Kumite: Sparring practice with a partner, simulating real combat situations.
