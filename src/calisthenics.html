Calisthenics

Calisthenics is a form of exercise consisting of a variety of movements that exercise large muscle groups (such as running, standing, grasping, pushing, etc.) and are often performed rhythmically and with minimal equipment.

Common calisthenics exercises include push-ups, pull-ups, sit-ups, squats, and planks. It can be performed indoors or outdoors, making it accessible to many people.

Benefits of calisthenics include improved strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular health, as well as the convenience of not needing a gym or specialized equipment.

Get started with calisthenics today and discover a versatile and effective way to improve your fitness!
